Vision-language grounding, on the edge

In progress

Fine-tuning and deploying vision-language models that locate objects in an image from a plain-language prompt — running entirely on an 8 GB, 15 W Jetson Orin Nano, no cloud. The method puts fidelity before hardware: choose the model spine, prove it survives quantization, then chase latency. Extended from single-frame grounding to holding a lock on a moving target through occlusion.
